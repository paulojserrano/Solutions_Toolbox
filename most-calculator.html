Maynard Operation Sequence Technique (MOST)

This tool utilizes BasicMOST concepts to calculate standard times. The calculations are based on the General Move sequence model, which accounts for approximately 60-70% of all manual work in logistics.

Sequence Model: A B G P

A (Action Distance) Horizontal movement (walking, reaching).
B (Body Motion) Vertical movement (bending, sitting).
G (Gain Control) Manual control (grasping).
P (Placement) Positioning the object (laying aside, fitting).
